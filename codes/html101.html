How to make lemonade

Instructions

  • Take a cup of water
  • Add a teaspoon of lemon juice
  • Add a pinch of salt
  • Add sugar/mishri/jaggery powder
  • Mix well for a minute

Lemonade is a refreshing drink that you can make in summers. Its rich in vitamin C and has many variations such as masala shikanji(indian recipe), mojito etc.
